## Why can't I change my profile details?

If you want to change your profile details, log out, change your details in your Google profile appropriately, and log back in. persona.fm uses Google's OAuth2.0 for authentication, and the profile details are fetched from your Google account. In the future, you will be able to change your profile details directly from persona.fm without changing your Google profile details.

## I want to delete my account

Please send an email to support@personafm.com with the subject "IMPORTANT - REQUEST TO DELETE ACCOUNT" and the email address associated with your account. 

THIS ACTION IS IRREVERSIBLE

All your data will be deleted from our servers, and you will not be able to recover it. Your Google account and it data will not be affected.
